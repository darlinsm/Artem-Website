What is Artem?

Artem aims to connect users with different artists and artworks in a quick and easy way. Many modern art databases are outdated, clunky, and provide the user with a terrible experience. It should be fun to look at art and discover new pieces, and so a website or application should not hold users back from doing so. With Artem, users will be able to discover new artists, artworks, and art genres all from their smart phone or computer. Furthermore, Google can sometimes be hit or miss when it comes to finding accurate information about art pieces. The goal of Artem is to create an art database that provides accurate information to user and can be accessed anywhere. Artem knows that it will likely fulfill a niche need, but it hopes that in doing so, it will be able to appeal to a much wider audience, who will be introduced to art in a whole new way.

Who uses Artem?

People use Artem to connect to art in a whole new way, to collect work that inspires them, and to learn about new kinds of artists from all kinds of periods. People cultivate mood boards of their favorite art pieces, learn about their favorite artists, and find accurate information to study all on Artem.
